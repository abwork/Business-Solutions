First project for the Integrify Spring Batch 2018 Implemented by Abdi Muleta.

The goal of the project is to assess the level of participants and refresh their memories. They will get a chance to use HTML and CSS while implementing this webpage. Participants are required to follow the design of the webpage as provided by the instructor.
